We are a local automotive repair service which operates in the District of Columbia Washington DC area. We are a full-service automotive repair shop that specializes in most European, Asian, domestic, hybrid and some electric vehicles. This includes all minor to major automotive service repairs.

Georgetown Auto Service is the Washington DC area automotive dealership alternative for all of your vehicle maintenance and repair needs. We provide exceptional quality service at a reasonable price. Here at Georgetown Auto Service, we strive to make your experience the best it can be for you and for your vehicle.

Our experienced technicians will diagnose your vehicle's problems and perform a technical Inspection. This enables you to know exactly what is wrong with your vehicle as well as any safety or maintenance issues that may exist.

Our facility is equipped with the latest in diagnostic equipment and scan tools to diagnose your vehicle and we will back it up with the most current training available. We only use factory or OEM replacement parts but also have access to quality aftermarket parts as well.

Your questions and concerns about your vehicle will be handled with the utmost care and respect by our experienced automotive service advisors and technicians. Since our founding in 2003, we continue to positively represent our name with quality work and honest pricing through our different automotive service.
